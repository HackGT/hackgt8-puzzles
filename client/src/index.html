What is this?

Congratulations! You have found our yearly admissions puzzle.

Every year we offer applicants the opportunity to try out interesting puzzles that we made the night before thoughtfully created.

What's the reward?

The first 50 applicants to complete all the puzzles will recieve automatic admission to HackGT 8.

Even if you don't finish in the top 50, each completion of a puzzle will earn you points that can be redeemed at the hackathon for prizes.

Solve Responsibly

We want you to have fun solving these puzzles. To ensure that everyone has a good time, we ask that you abide by the following rules for the puzzles:

  • Solve the puzzles using your brain. Any attempts to gain an unfair advantage in completing the puzzles will not be tolerated. You may not attempt to or gain unauthorized access to any non-public information related to the puzzles, including but not limited to HackGT's application servers, the puzzles' source code, and the solutions.

  • No sharing solutions. You may not share solutions to the puzzles in any form, publicly or privately.

  • The puzzles are an individual exercise. Solve the puzzles on your own! A limited amount of collaboration is allowed when completing the puzzles. You are allowed to share hints and offer vague help to others. However, sharing solutions is never allowed. If we believe excessive collaboration is occurring, anyone involved will have their results invalidated. We reserve the right to monitor for cheating both manually and using automated technologies.

By following these three simple rules, we hope everyone can have a great experience with the HackGT 8 puzzles. However, if we believe you have violated any of these rules, we reserve the right to take any and all necessary corrective actions, including but not limited to:

  • Asking that you take down solutions that were posted and accessible to others
  • Disqualifying you from any admissions bonuses or automatic acceptances related to the puzzles
  • Disqualifying you from HackGT 8
By participating (or continuing to participate) in the HackGT 8 puzzles, you agree to this policy
